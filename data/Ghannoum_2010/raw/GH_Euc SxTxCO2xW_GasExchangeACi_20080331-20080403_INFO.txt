Gas exchange, CO2 response curves, made at two temperatures (28 and 34C)
temperature x CO2 x species 

Variables

Date
Time
Species: Eucalyptus species	
Temp: air temperature treatment
CO2: carbon dioxide treatment (units:  µL L-1 CO2)	
Potnum: pot number
MeasTemp: LI-6400 block temperature that the measurement was made at

The rest of the variables are standard LI-6400
