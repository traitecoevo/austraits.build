Wollemi pine
Material from destructive harvest, dried at 70C, ground and a subsample was sent to Iker (Renee)

For further information relating to plant growing conditions and treatments, refer to experimental text file

Based on the file "GHS30_WollemiTxCO2_Isotopes_20100503_L1.csv"

Data owner: David Tissue
Personnel who collected the data: Iker Aranjuelo
Personnel who worked on data set: Iker Aranjuelo

Publications: manuscript in prep


Variables

Temp: air temperature treatment	
CO2: carbon dioxide treatment (units:  µL L-1 CO2)
Organ: plant part i.e. leaves, roots or stem 	
Potnum: pot number		
N: % nitrogen
d15N: nitrogen isotope
CN: carbon to nitrogen ratio
C: % carbon
d13C: carbon isotope
